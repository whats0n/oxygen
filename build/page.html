Matched conditions from the two parties (the borrower and the lender) activate the deal in the public REPO contract. Activation means that the two parties agree on all terms, including:

  • Type and amount of borrowed token (ERC20)
  • Length of the deal
  • Type of token used as collateral
  • Interest rate

A placement request is initiated by an asset holder willing to lend a specific amount of his asset. While it can be generated automatically by the matching engine, any interested party can interact with the REPO contract directly on the Ethereum network by sending transactions.

The asset holder must specify:

  • Type and amount of token he wants to lend
  • Desirable tokens and quantity of each to use as a collateral
  • Minimum credit rating of the borrower
  • Length of the deal
  • Interest rate (overall)
  • Whether his deal can be split into smaller deals
  • Expiration time of the proposal
  • Minimal rating of the borrower

A loan request is initiated by a borrower who needs a specific ERC20 token and can offer another token(s) in security for the loan deal. While loan requests can also be processed automatically by the matching engine, any party can invoke the REPO contract to directly post a deal proposal.

The borrower must specify:

  • Desired ERC20 token
  • Quantity of token
  • Length of the deal
  • Which ERC20 token(s) the borrower will use as a collateral (and the quantity of each)
  • Expiration time of the proposal

Part of the continuous real-time auction. We provide not only matching services, but also the dissemination of price-forks to participants based on models that incorporate both book-value and market-data pricing models.

The risk management toolkit uses data collected from all previous exchange deals to provide advice on future deals.

A set of helpful web applications and decentralized apps that help participants to interact with the decentralized REPO contract.

The core of decentralized REPO deals. While heavily used by the matching engine, any party can use this smart-contract directly on Ethereum network.

The credit rating is decentralised and managed by another smart-contract on Ethereum network. Every participant’s rating starts at 0 and can be increased by completing deals as the borrower. Asset holders looking to lend their ERC20 tokens can specify the minimum desired rating of the borrower in their placements.

Etherdelta is a decentralized exchange. When the collateral goes through, the original asset holder might prefer (optional) to get assets in the original tokens that he was borrowing rather than the collateral tokens. This is done using Etherdelta.

The system fee is a small ETH value included in any transaction to the REPO contract.

A loan is given by the asset holder who wishes to lend his assets for a specific amount of time and rate of interest.

The collateral is the agreed amount of ERC20 tokens used in security for the loan deal.

If the borrower fails to return borrowed tokens when the deal has expired, the deal is automatically considered default. The lender will then be able to claim the collateral from the contract.

When the borrower successfully returns borrowed tokens with interests while the deal is still active (not expired), the deal is automatically considered complete.

The borrower rating is updated according to the outcome of the deal.

  • In case of default deal, it goes down.
  • Rating change is proportionate to the volume of the deal.
  • Rating of all participants is stored in a separate contract.

The borrower rating is updated according to the outcome of the deal.

  • In case of completed deal, it goes up.
  • Rating change is proportionate to the volume of the deal.
  • Rating of all participants is stored in a separate contract.

In case of default deal, collateral is claimed by the lender. It can go through the decentralized Etherdelta exchange to convert into lender-preferred tokens. This operation is optional and can be chosen by lender during smart contract creation.

In case of completed deal, interests and borrowed tokens are transferred to the lender. It can go through the decentralized Etherdelta exchange to convert into lender-preferred tokens.This operation is optional and can be chosen by lender during smart contract creation.

Interest is an agreed percentage of the collateral assets. The lender claims it when the deal is closed gracefully.

In case of default, repayment is the process of transferring collateral tokens locked in the REPO Contract to the Lender. This leads to 100% secure refund.

Original tokens the Borrower used according to the deal are returned to the Lender fully using the ERC20 contract interface.

When the deal is closed gracefully, the collateral is unblocked from the REPO contract and is returned to the borrower. Interests are payed to the lender.

Key features

The following cutting-edge technologies support market creation of tokens and coins, which is beneficial for traders and cryptocurrency stakeholders.

Blockchain-recorded credit rating

The REPO Ethereum-based smart-contract updates decentralized credit rating, whether the loan is complete or default. The platform will feature a transparent algorithm to avoid fraud and build a trustworthy market.

Community-driven orderbook

Lenders and borrowers push requests to a dynamically generated orderbook. Continuous auction results in several matched requests. Users can choose to lock down their assets, as secured requests match faster.

Joint loans

Lenders and borrowers are also able to participate in joint loans by accepting requests in part. Joint loans are available for secured requests if selected by the owner upon request setup.

AI-based risk management toolkit

Oxygen neural network aggregates plenty of data sources and offers indicative rates, default-risk coefficients, discount factors and other market data to provide advisory on accurate estimate about a potential offer.

Truly decentralized platform

Oxygen delivers a fully decentralized collateral acquisition and settlement system. There is no centralized custody at any stage of user exchange, guaranteeing lenders and borrowers unbreakable fund safety.

B2B Integrations available

Today's cryptocurrency exchanges face a lack of REPO instruments and cannot offer safe short positions and margin trading to their clients. Oxygen prioritises the development and implementation of SAAS applications for businesses.

Oxygen experience

The Oxygen platform and user experience are designed for intuitive loan management. Members can collateralize their blockchain assets, manage repayments and monitor activity in one easy-to-use platform.

Dashboard

Oxygen’s user homepage gives quick access to personal loaning. Users can easily check the status of active lending and borrowing contracts, and understand at a glance the amount of all crypto-assets available considering current margin trading operations.
