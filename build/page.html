Key features

Following cutting-edge technologies support market creation of tokens and coins, beneficial for traders and cryptocurrency stakeholders.

Credit rating

Ethereum-based smart-contract of REPO updates decentralised credit rating, whether the loan was completed successfully or default occurred.

Community-driven orderbook

Lenders and borrowers push requests to dynamically generated orderbook. Continuous auction results in a number of matched requests. Secured requests are matching faster.

Joint loans

Lenders and borrowers are able to participate in joint loans. Joint loans are available for secured requests when it was committed by the owner on request setup.

AI-based risk management

Oxygen neural network aggregates plenty of data sources and offers indicative rates, default-risk coefficients, discount factors and other market advisory.

Truly decentralized platform

Oxygen delivers a fully decentralized collateral acquisition and settlement system. There is no centralized custody at any stage of swap between users.

B2B Integrations

Development and implementation of SAAS applications for businesses is a high priority for Oxygen core team.

Oxygen experience

The Oxygen platform and user experience are designed for intuitive loan management. Members can collateralize their blockchain assets, manage repayments and monitor activity in one easy to use platform.

Dashboard

Oxygen user homepage gives quick access to personal loaning. Users can easily check the stage of active lending and borrowing contracts and understand at a glance the amount of all crypto assets available considering current margin trading operations.
