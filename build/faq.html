OXYGEN FAQ

About the platform

What is Oxygen?

Oxygen is an analog to the REPO market in the cryptocurrency world. It is built of Ethereum smart contracts to provide secure of collateral and client-side applications, which hold private keys. Oxygen will allow token holders to earn profit from safe allocation of their cryptoassets, while other token users and traders will be able to do short-selling and margin trading with cryptoassets borrowed for token collateral.

What services will Oxygen provide?

Oxygen will perform REPO operations in ERC20 tokens and Ethereum platform. We are considering to extend it to BTC and other cryptographic assets, but no formal commitment can be made at this stage of the project, due to technological lag in true cross-chain solutions. Essentially, Oxygen will provide a specialized peer-to-peer environment for lending assets specifically for trading purposes.

Why does Oxygen go beyond peer-to-peer lending?

Peer-to-peer trading is an analog to consumer/commercial credit agreements and therefore does not address the specific needs and risks associated with trading. While Oxygen can be used for peer-to-peer lending, it is aimed at a different economic activity, due to a specialized set of rules, and therefore may or may not be suitable for certain transactions.

How is Oxygen different from the short at Poloniex or Bitfinex?

Oxygen is neither a short nor a long position (even though some synthetic operations may work with long and short positions). Oxygen is a tool enabling token holders to offer margin trading to third parties, regardless of the margin being a long or a short one.

How is Oxygen different from Bancor?

Bancor positions itself as a market-maker and supply/demand management system for token holders. This is not related to margin trading or any activities beyond spot trading.

How decentralized and secure is Oxygen?

Oxygen is a fully decentralized platform deployed in the Ethereum network. There is no central custody in-between transaction participants, due to usage of smart contracts. All private keys are held by client-side applications and Ethereum network provides collateral security.

Why does the Oxygen environment consist of ERC20 tokens only?

Right now there is no feasible and impartial cross-chain solution which would allow secure operations with both ETH and BTC blockchains. On the other hand, for now, Ethereum is probably the most popular platform to perform ICO, and dozens of tokens are being issued in the ERC20 standard. Moreover, ERC20-capable tokens are essential for transactions based on Ethereum smart contracts. These are the main reasons why we primarily focus on the ERC20 standard. However, we want to go cross-chain as soon as a true and reliable solution appears.

What is an order book?

An order book is a list of bids and asks placed by all parties that wish to lend or borrow a given asset. In the case of Oxygen, an order book is a centralized exchange built on the Disruptor technology, also used by LMAX. It will run a continuous auction with the books representing different tokens and different expiry blocks. Importantly, it will not be fully anonymous — orders will be highlighted with various colors, depending on the participant’s credit rating.

About our business model

How do I use Oxygen for my business?

Oxygen is designed to unlock the hidden potential of your cryptoassets. Basically, you can pledge some of your tokens as a collateral and get a loan you need, or lend them and get your interest rate. But Oxygen is also a powerful tool for long-term token holders who can earn profit from lending their crypto assets. Experienced traders and it could be used for the way different cases of margin trading. If you envision a co-operation with us in this sphere, we’d love to hear about your project. You can reach us at hello@oxygen.trade.

How does the platform earn profit?

Users pay a fee for each transaction. The fee amount depends on transactions' terms and the amount of collateralized and lent tokens.

About crowdfunding

How do tokens work?

Tokens play a significant role in the Oxygen platform. They will not only be used to cover Oxygen’s fees, but will also open the following benefits to token holders:

  • Deals in OXG tokens under more advantageous conditions (an up to 50 % rebate)
  • Priority in the order book queue (in case the credit rating permits it)
  • Last look during trades
  • Liabilities coverage to avoid penalty in case of a credit event
  • Right to vote on implementation of new features

Pre-sale contributors will get an up to 20% bonus.

What will you use the funds for?

20 % of funds will be used for development of the platform and legal activities.

25% of funds will be used for promotion of Oxygen in Asia and globally.

5 % of funds will reserved to cover unexpected costs.
