A deal is defined as an event when both counterparties are registered by the matching and settlement is triggered by the smart contract under following conditions:

  • Type and amount of borrowed ERC20 tokens
  • Deal duration
  • Type of tokens used as a collateral
  • Interest rate

The lender must specify:

  • Asset and size to be offered to borrowers
  • Collateral requirements
  • Borrower's minimum credit rating
  • Contract maturity
  • Interest rate
  • Allow pooled offers
  • Proposal expiration

The borrower must specify:

  • Asset and quantity seeked
  • Contract maturity
  • Collateral he is ready to post
  • Proposal expiration time

The matching engine has no access to private keys.

Intelligent system provides advisory on market - ceiling, floors and indicative rates for offer submission based on market events.

A set of web applications where participants interact with decentralized REPO contracts.

The core of decentralized REPO transaction. Security of assets is provided by Ethereum network. The smart contract acts as the settlement mechanism for deals that occurred through the public auction.

The rating rewards and penalizes users proportionally to the deal’s volumes. A separate smart contract stores the rating data.

{# #}

A treasury management streamlining feature. The lender can specify that he wants the process of collateral realization to be automatically performed through Etherdelta. In such event, orders are automatically placed and the deal’s proceeds are directly transferred to the the lender.

System fee is paid in OXG tokens. Gas included.

Loan conditions are defined as an interest rate from notional value of borrowed asset.

The collateral is held by the smart contract which then delivers it to either party depending on the credit-event.

If the borrower fails to return borrowed tokens when the deal has expired, the deal is automatically considered default. The lender will then be able to claim the collateral from the contract.

When the borrower successfully returns borrowed tokens with interests while the deal is still active (not expired), the deal is automatically considered complete.

In case of a default the rating declines.

In case of a successfully closed loan the rating increases.

OPTIONAL
If the lender has specified this clause in the contract – automated realization.

Optional. If the lender has specified this clause in the contract the interest is paid from the collateral.

Interest is an agreed percentage of borrowed assets.

All transactions are secured and the lender will always receive the collateral in case of borrower’s default on payment.

Borrowed assets are returned to the lender using the ERC20 contract interface.

The collateral is unblocked from the REPO contract and is returned to the borrower.
Support of all ERC20-compliant tokens.
