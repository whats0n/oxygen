Introducing Oxygen

Oxygen is introducing to the cryptocurrency paradigm something similar to the REPO market—clear, simple and efficient. It is designed to assist you to get the most out of your digital assets and offers support for all ERC20 compliant tokens.

Matched conditions from two parties (Borrower and Lender) activate the deal in the public REPO Contract. Activation means that two parties agree on all terms including:

  • Amount of borrowed token
  • Length of the deal
  • Type of the token used as collateral
  • Interest rate
